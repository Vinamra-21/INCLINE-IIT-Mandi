About Us

INCLINE (Indian Climate Information Explorer) is a comprehensive decision support platform designed to enhance climate resilience across India. This state-of-the-art system integrates climate data, advanced analytics, and user-friendly tools to help both decision-makers and local communities understand and respond to climate challenges effectively. INCLINE provides crucial insights for extreme weather management, long-term climate planning, and sector-specific adaptation strategies. Developed by the HIMPACT Lab at IIT Mandi, the platform serves diverse users from local community members to government officials, urban planners, disaster management authorities, and sector experts - enabling informed climate-smart decisions for a resilient India.

Our Mission

INCLINE's mission is to democratize climate information and empower informed decision-making at all levels of Indian society. We strive to bridge the gap between complex climate science and practical action by providing accessible, user-friendly tools and information. Our goal is to enable both local communities and decision-makers to better understand, prepare for, and respond to climate challenges, ultimately contributing to a climate-resilient India.

VISION

To be India's leading platform for accessible climate information and decision support, fostering climate-resilient communities through knowledge-driven action.

Key Objectives

Our key objectives focus on enhancing climate resilience and supporting sustainable practices.

Key Objectives:

  • ✔ Make climate information accessible and understandable for all users
  • ✔ Support evidence-based decision-making for climate adaptation
  • ✔ Enable better management of climate extremes and associated risks
  • ✔ Facilitate local-level climate action and community resilience
  • ✔ Bridge the gap between scientific knowledge and practical implementation

Technology & Innovation

INCLINE is built using modern web technologies to provide you with an easy-to-use climate information platform. Our system processes high-quality climate data from multiple reliable sources including global climate models and observed records to give you accurate insights about India's climate.

Key Features:

  • ✔ Generate location-specific climate projections for any place in India
  • ✔ View easy-to-understand maps and graphs
  • ✔ Compare climate conditions across different time periods
  • ✔ Get watershed information for your region
  • ✔ Access comprehensive climate reports (coming soon)

Future Developments:

We are continuously working to add new features including:
  • ✔ Drought early warning system
  • ✔ Additional climate variables and indicators
  • ✔ Enhanced visualization tools
